Pilgrimmage Site

Takes two parameters.

First parameter must be a book, a pot, or an ornament. Second parameter must also be a book, a pot, or an ornament -- however this could be something that was produced by the first parameter.

Example:

U(F36,Or,Or)

Turns 2 ornaments into 2 reliquaries.

U(F36,Po,Or)

Turns 1 pot into 1 reliquary (there is an intermediate ornament).

U(F36,Bo,)

Convert 1 book into 1 pot.

U(F36,,Bo)

Convert 1 book into 1 pot.
