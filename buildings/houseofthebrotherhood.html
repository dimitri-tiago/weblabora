House of the Brotherhood

Gives you points depending on the number of cloister buildings on your landscape.

Format is U(F21,input,output)

Examples:

U(F21,Ni,Rq)

Eats your Nickel. Gives you a Reliquary (provided you have at least 4 cloister buildings)

U(F21,PnPnPnPnWn,BoPo)

Converts 4 pennies and 1 wine (used as a coin) into a book and a pottery (5 points). The 6th point here would be wasted, might be a good idea to get two pottery, but maybe you want that book for the Sacristy?
