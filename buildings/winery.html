Winery

Turns grapes into wine, and a single wine into 7 coins (which you usually want to do). It can also just convert a wine into 7 coins.

Examples:

U(F21,GpGpGp,Wn)

Converts 3 grapes into 3 wine, but consumes 1 of those wine to give back 7 coins.

U(F21,,Wn)

Doesn't convert any grapes into wine, but turns a wine into 7 coins. The double comma is important.

U(F21,GpGpGpGp,)

Converts 4 grapes into 4 wine. Does not turn any of those wines into coins. The trailing comma is important.
