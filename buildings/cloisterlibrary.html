Cloister Library

Converts up to 3 coins into 3 books. It can also convert 1 book into a meat and a wine. Awww yeah.

Example:

U(F17,PnPnPn,Bo)

Input: 3 pennies. Output: 2 books, 1 meat, 1 wine.

U(F17,Ni,Bo)

Input: 1 nickel. Output: 2 pennies, 2 books, 1 meat, 1 wine. It automatically changes the nickel into 5 pennies and assumes 3 pennies into 3 books. Then converts 1 book into meat and wine somehow.

U(F17,Pn,)

Converts 1 penny into 1 book. That's all. The trailing comma is important.

U(F17,Pn,Bo)

Converts 1 penny into 1 meat and 1 wine.

U(F17,,Bo)

Converts 1 book into 1 meat and 1 wine. The double comma is important.
